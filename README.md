Wending Machine Simulator

A dark comedy capitalist vending machine survival simulator.

Run a vending empire in a city slowly optimized into a corporate consumption ecosystem. Expand your machine network, survive hostile corporations, manipulate supply chains, manage reputation, and try not to collapse under the weight of your own success.

Or do. The city barely notices anymore.
